The way to the soul is through a cup of tea!

Tea is not just a beverage in India-it is an emotion. It is the ultimate remedy that not only relieves stress but also soothes the soul. From chai stalls in the smallest street corners to high-end malls, Indians are seen sipping on a hot cup of tea everywhere.

The deeply rooted love for India’s favourite beverage paved the way for the inception of Tea Stall- a place dedicated to chai lovers! The fastest-growing tea chain in India, Tea Stall is responsible for serving more than 2 million cups of happiness every month in the form of an aromatic cup of tea. Even though it is a popular beverage across the country, chai lovers could not find a café that served an array of teas at an affordable price. To bridge this gap, Tea Stall crafted cafés and outlets that could cater to the common man and his love for enjoying a good cup of tea in the middle of his hectic day, served with a side of delectable snacks.

Guarantee of qualitea!

We understand the importance of a good cup of tea- we really do! At Tea Stall, we ensure that every cup served is freshly brewed and meets the hallmarks of taste and quality every single time. The secret to a good cup of tea is the love of the person who makes it. Therefore, we have a team of experienced chai makers who ensure that each cup is filled with love! The ambience and sanitation at Tea Stall are of utmost importance to us. When you visit Tea Stall, you find a clean, hygienic place to sit and have great conversations with friends and family over a cup of tea, delicious snacks and the promise of the best quality.

An ode to the society

Since its inception in 2013, Tea Stall has garnered the love of millions of Indians who have welcomed us into their lives with open arms. To demonstrate our love for society, Tea Stall created the Tea Stall Well Wish Foundation. Our intention is to be able to impact lives in society and works towards the betterment and upliftment of the community as a whole.
